<p><p>The mass spectrometry detector market encompasses various types, primarily including Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (GC-MS), Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (LC-MS), and others. GC-MS combines gas chromatography for volatile compounds with mass spectrometry for molecular identification, making it ideal for environmental and industrial applications. LC-MS utilizes liquid chromatography for complex samples, widely used in pharmaceuticals and biochemistry. Other methods include tandem mass spectrometry and ion mobility spectrometry, catering to specialized analytical needs across diverse industries, including healthcare and food safety.</p></p>
