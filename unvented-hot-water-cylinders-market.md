<p><strong>The Unvented Hot Water Cylinders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Heating</li><li>Indirect Heating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Unvented hot water cylinders are classified into two main market types: direct heating and indirect heating. Direct heating relies on an electric immersion heater to warm the water directly within the cylinder, providing a quick response to hot water demand. In contrast, indirect heating uses an external heat source, such as a boiler, to heat a fluid that warms the water in the cylinder. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to different installation preferences and heating system compatibility.</p></p>
