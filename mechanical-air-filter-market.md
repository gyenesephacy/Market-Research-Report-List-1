<p><p>The mechanical air filter market comprises several types, including Pre-Filters, Sub-HEPA Filters, and Secondary Filters. Pre-Filters are designed to capture larger particles, prolonging the life of subsequent filters. Sub-HEPA Filters provide enhanced filtration, capturing smaller particles than standard filters but not reaching the HEPA standard. Secondary Filters act as a final line of defense, ensuring maximum airflow and particle removal, often used in conjunction with primary filters to improve overall air quality in various environments.</p></p>
