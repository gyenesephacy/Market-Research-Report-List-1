<p><strong>The Home Fire Sprinklers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet</li><li>Dry</li><li>Preaction</li><li>Deluge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home fire sprinkler market includes various types designed for different applications: </p><p>1. **Wet Systems** keep water in the pipes, ready for immediate discharge during a fire.</p><p>2. **Dry Systems** use pressurized air to prevent water in pipes, ideal for cold climates.</p><p>3. **Preaction Systems** require activation from both smoke detection and heat to release water, blending wet and dry features for enhanced safety.</p><p>4. **Deluge Systems** release water from open sprinklers simultaneously, suitable for high-risk areas needing rapid suppression.</p></p>
