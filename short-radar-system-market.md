<p><strong>The Short Radar System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CW Radars</li><li>Pulsed Radars</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Short radar systems are classified primarily into two types: Continuous Wave (CW) radars and Pulsed radars. CW radars emit a continuous signal, allowing for precise speed detection, making them ideal for applications like traffic enforcement and law enforcement. In contrast, pulsed radars transmit short bursts of energy, enabling them to determine distances and detect objects over longer ranges. Each type serves unique applications in aviation, maritime, and military sectors, addressing varying requirements for accuracy, range, and environment adaptability.</p></p>
