<p><ul><li>Isokinetic Operation</li><li>Frequency Conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Spiral Escalator market can be categorized into two main types based on operational mechanisms: Isokinetic Operation and Frequency Conversion. Isokinetic Operation maintains a constant speed across the escalator, ensuring smooth and uniform passenger movement, ideal for settings requiring steady flow. In contrast, Frequency Conversion allows for adjustable speeds, enhancing energy efficiency and adapting to varying traffic demands. Both types aim to improve user experience and operational efficiency while catering to different installation environments and user needs.</p></p>
