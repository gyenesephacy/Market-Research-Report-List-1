<p><p>The solar charge controller market features primarily two types: PWM (Pulse Width Modulation) and MPPT (Maximum Power Point Tracking) controllers. PWM controllers regulate voltage and current in a simpler, cost-effective manner, suitable for smaller systems. In contrast, MPPT controllers optimize the power output from solar panels by adjusting the electrical operating point, maximizing efficiency, particularly in larger installations. The growing adoption of renewable energy drives their demand, with MPPT controllers commanding a premium due to their advanced capabilities.</p></p>
