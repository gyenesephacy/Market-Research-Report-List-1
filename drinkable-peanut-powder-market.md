<p><ul><li>Soluble (Powder)</li><li>Insoluble (Particle)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drinkable peanut powder market is divided into two primary types: soluble (powder) and insoluble (particle). Soluble peanut powder dissolves easily in liquids, making it ideal for smoothies, shakes, or beverages, promoting quick absorption of nutrients. In contrast, insoluble peanut powder retains its particle form, providing texture and a heartier mouthfeel, suitable for recipes like protein bars and baked goods. Each type offers distinct benefits, catering to diverse consumer preferences and applications in the food and beverage industry.</p></p>
